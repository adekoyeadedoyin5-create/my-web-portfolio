Homemade Garlic Butter Pasta

A quick, comforting, and deeply flavorful pasta dish perfect for busy weeknights.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Servings: 4 people
Calories: 420 kcal

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 50g Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Parsley

Directions

  1. Cook the Pasta

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.

  2. Make the Sauce

    While pasta cooks, melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Combine and Serve

    Drain pasta, reserving 1/4 cup of pasta water. Toss pasta into the garlic butter skillet. Add parmesan and parsley, stirring until creamy.
